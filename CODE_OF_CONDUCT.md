# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not individuals.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive or bad-faith interactions.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.
